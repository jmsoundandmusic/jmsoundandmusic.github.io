About

Hey there! Thanks for visiting my website!

My name is Jorge M. (or George) and I have been interested in the world of music, sound and media since I remember. It all started when I was a kid and discovered the electric guitar. Fascinated by the interesting sounds of rock and metal, I spent most of my free time playing and learning the instrument. Soon after, I started to expand my music interests and to produce my own music. In order to combine music with other interests of mine like films and videogames, I decided to study Music Producion and Media Studies.

My work experience includes recording, editing and mixing of several short films, comercials and radio podcasts and teaching electric guitar.

I also love learning about music tecnology and gear, both software and hardware: synths, effects, guitar pedals and amps, etc. Nowadays I am focused on increasing my knowledge in audio implementation for videogames and in learning DSP (Digital Signal Processing) to create audio plugins.

I am a curious mind and apart of making music, sound, and playing videogames, I also enjoy learning about science and programming.
